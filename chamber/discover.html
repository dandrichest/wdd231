Discover us

Central Park Abuja

Kur Mohammed Avenue, Plot 174, Abuja, Federal Capital Territory, 9001

A vibrant theme park offering activities like go-karting, paintball, mini-golf, archery, and more.

Nike Art Gallery Abuja

Kilometre 7.5 Abuja International Airport Road, Piwoyi Village, Abuja

A cultural hub showcasing Nigerian art, workshops, and crafts.

Millennium Park

Maitama District, Abuja

A serene green space ideal for picnics, leisurely walks, and sightseeing. The park features sculptures, art installations, and beautiful landscapes.

Jabi Lake

Jabi District, Abuja

A picturesque lake offering activities like boat rides, fishing, and picnics. Surrounded by restaurants and cafes, making it a great spot for relaxation

Magicland Amusement Park

Constitution Avenue, Abuja

A family-friendly amusement park with rides, games, and activities for all ages. A great spot for kids and adults alike.

Aso Rock

Near the Presidential Villa, Abuja

A massive rock formation symbolizing Nigeria’s political power. Visitors can take guided tours to learn about its history and significance.

Abuja Arts and Crafts Village

Central Business District, Abuja

A vibrant market showcasing Nigerian crafts, textiles, and souvenirs. A great place to shop for unique items and experience local culture.

Thought Pyramid Art Centre

18 Libreville Crescent, Wuse II, Abuja

A contemporary art gallery showcasing a wide range of Nigerian and African art. A must-visit for art enthusiasts.
